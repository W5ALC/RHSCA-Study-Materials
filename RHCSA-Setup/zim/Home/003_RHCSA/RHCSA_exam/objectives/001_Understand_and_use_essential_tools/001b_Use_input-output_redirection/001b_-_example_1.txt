**KYWRD:** 001b - example 1
**RHCSA Objective: **001b Use input-output redirection

**Summary**
 Open a terminal on a CentOS/RHEL machine, echo "Hello World", and redirect it to a file called hello_world.txt.

**001b - example 1 - tasks **(command line commands are __highlighted__)**:**
* Startup your CentOS/RHEL machine, and login as a normal user.
* Redirect the output of 'echo "Hello World" to Examples/hello_world.txt: __echo "Hello World" > Examples/hello_world.txt__
* Verify that hello_world.txt was created by inspecting the output of this command: __find Examples__
* View the contents of hello_world.txt: __cat Examples/hello_world.txt__
