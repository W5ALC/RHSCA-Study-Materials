**KYWRD:** unsetenv
**RHCSA Objective:** 001a Access a shell prompt and issue commands with correct syntax

**Summary:**
The **setenv** command in Linux/Unix is used to add or change environment variables.  Its opposite command, **unsetenv**, is used to delete variable names from the environment.
