**RHCSA Objective: **001a Access a shell prompt and issue commands with correct syntax

**Summary:**
**bg** is a Linux/Unix utility that is used to restart and suspended job. Once restarted, the job runs in the background.

**bg:**
* bg - function: Restart suspended jobs, and runs them in the background.
