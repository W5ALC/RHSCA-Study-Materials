**RHCSA Objective: **003b Create and remove physical volumes, assign physical volumes to volume groups, and create and delete logical volumes

**Summary**
The badblocks utility is used to test storage devices for bad blocks.

**badblocks:**
* badblocks - function: test storage devices for bad blocks.
* badblocks - description: search for bad blocks, typically on a disk partition.
