**RHCSA Objective: **001a Access a shell prompt and issue commands with correct syntax

**Summary:**
cal - example 1 demonstrates how to correctly use the **cal** command.

**Scenario:**
Bose is a RHEL sysadmin at Google.  She wants to display a calendar of the current month with the current day highlighted. What command will she issue?

**cal - example 1 - tasks **(command line commands are __highlighted__)**:**
1. Startup your CentOS/RHEL system, and login as a normal user.
2. At a command prompt, issue the cal command: __cal__
