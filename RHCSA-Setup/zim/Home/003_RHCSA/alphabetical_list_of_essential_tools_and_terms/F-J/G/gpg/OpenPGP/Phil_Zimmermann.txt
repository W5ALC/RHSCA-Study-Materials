Phil Zimmermann
* born February 12, 1954
* created [[P-T:P:Pretty Good Privacy]] ([[P-T:P:PGP]]), widely used email encryption software
* follows the :gpg:OpenPGP:OpenPGP standard for encrypting/decrypting data defined in  :gpg:OpenPGP:OpenPGP:RFC 4880
