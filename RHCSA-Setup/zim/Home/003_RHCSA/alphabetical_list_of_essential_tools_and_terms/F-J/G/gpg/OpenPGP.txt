====== OpenPGP ======
Created Tuesday 17 July 2018

:Home:RHCSA:alphabetical list of essential tools and terms:F-J:G:gpg:gpg

OpenPGP is a popular email encryption standard:
* defined by the OpenPGP Working Group of the [[+Internet Engineering Task Force]] ([[I:IETF]])
* was a proposed standard in [[+RFC 4880]]
* was derived from [[+Phil Zimmermann|Phil Zimmerman's]] PGP software

More information on OpenPGP can be found here: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pretty_Good_Privacy#OpenPGP
