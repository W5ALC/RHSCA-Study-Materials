**Summary**
1 gigabyte (GB) of space listed in the du and df utlilities equals all of the following:
* 1 [[gigibyte]] 
* 2^30 bytes
* 1024^3 bytes
* 1,073,741,824 bytes

The textbook definition of a gigabyte is **10^9 bytes**.
The textbook definition of a gigibyte is **2^30 bytes**.
