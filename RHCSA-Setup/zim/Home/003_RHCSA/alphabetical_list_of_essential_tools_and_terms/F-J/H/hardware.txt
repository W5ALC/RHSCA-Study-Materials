**Summary:**
Computer operating systems, such as RHEL or CentOS, have to be able handle hardware from a variety of vendors.  CentOS has a hardware support forum here https://www.centos.org/forums/viewforum.php?f=49 
Red Hat maintains a hardware compatibility list here: https://access.redhat.com/ecosystem/search/#/category/Server

**hardware:**
* hardware - function: computer hardware consists of the physical components that allow a computer to function. This includes the keyboard, mouse, monitor, CPU, RAM etc.
