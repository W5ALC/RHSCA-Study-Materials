**Summary:**
A **hard drive** is formally known as a **hard disk drive** (HDD).  It is also known as a **hard disk**, or **fixed disk**.  The magnetic properties of rotating disks (known as platters), and magnetics heads are used to store and retrieve data on the disks.

A hard drive is comprised of four major parts:
1. [[+actuator]]
2. [[+heads]]
3. [[+platters]]
4. [[+spindle]]
