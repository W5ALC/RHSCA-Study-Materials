**RHCSA Objective: **001f Archive, compress, unpack, and uncompress files using tar, star, gzip, and bzip2

**Summary**
This article details the options that are available with the [[star]] command.

**star - options:**
* -c
	* creates a new tarfile and writes named files to it
	* writing starts at the beginning of the tarfile
	* verbosity can be increased by adding the -v (verbose) option
	* Home:RHCSA:alphabetical list of essential tools and terms:P-T:S:star -c deep dive
* -v
	* tells star to operate in verbose mode
* -f=filename or -file=filename
	* tells star the desired filename of the tarfile that it is going to create
