**RHCSA Objective: **5.a Configure networking and hostname resolution statically or dynamically

**Summary**
Network traffic shaping is done in the Linux kernel using the [[tc]] utility Shaping is used to control the rate of data transmission. Shaping may consist of lowering available bandwidth AND/OR smoothing out bursts to provide more stable network behavior. Shaping occurs on egress.
