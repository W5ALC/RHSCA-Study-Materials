**Summary**
The tc utility is part of the iproute2 network control suite. It is used to show and manipulate network traffic control settings. tc stands for "Traffic Control", and it consists of four components:
* [[+tc shaping]]
* [[+tc scheduling]]
* [[+tc policing]]
* [[+tc dropping]]
