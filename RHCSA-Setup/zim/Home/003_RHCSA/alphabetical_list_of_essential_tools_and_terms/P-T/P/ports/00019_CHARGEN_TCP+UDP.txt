**KYWRD:** CHARGEN
**RHCSA Objective: **002h Start, stop, and check the status of network services

**Summary:**
CHARGEN stands for **Character Generator Protocol.** CHARGEN is used to send an endless stream of characters for testing, debugging and measurement purposes. It uses **TCP/UDP ports 19**.

**CHARGEN:**
* CHARGEN - function: CHARGEN sends an endless stream of characters which are used for testing, debugging and measurement purposes.
