**KYWRD:** QOTD
**RHCSA Objective: **002h Start, stop, and check the status of network services

**Summary:**
QOTD stands for **Quote of the Day**. It serves up daily quotes to connected hosts, and it is also used for testing and measurement. QOTD uses **TCP/UDP ports 17**.

**QOTD:**
* QOTD - function: serves up daily quotes to connected hosts, and also provides for testing and measurement.
* QOTD - description: PN/A
* QOTD - options-flags: see ncat.
* QOTD - examples: see ncat.

* QOTD service can be provided by the ncat utility.
