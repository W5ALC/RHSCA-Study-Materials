**KYWRD:** TCPMUX
**RHCSA Objective: **002h Start, stop, and check the status of network services

**Summary:**
TCPMUX stands for **TCP Port Service Multiplexer**. It provides a TCP port multiplexing service. TCPMUX sses **TCP port 1**.


**TCPMUX:**
* TCPMUX - function: Clients use TCP port 1 to contact a remote host for multiple services.
